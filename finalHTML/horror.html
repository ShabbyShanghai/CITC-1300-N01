HORROR

My Favorite Horror Movies

Since halloween is coming up and I love horror movies, I thought I would recommend some movies to watch.

I'm only going to do the best of each of their own category with brief reasons on why they're there.

Slasher

Halloween

The original Halloween defined the slasher genre.It inspired many others such as Friday the 13, Child's play, and Scream(in a different way.) Because it's my favorite doesn't mean I think it's the best, but it is great.

Sci-Fi Horror

The Thing

It's hard to talk about the movie without spoiling it so if you haven't I highly recommend to go watch it.But it is a rollercoaster and it has so many twists and turn. Is the definitive halloween movie.

Psychological Horror

The Shining

The Shining is another movie that if you haven't watched it, you should. It's a great movie even if Stephen King doesn't like it (for some reason. Yes it gets a few things wrong, but it's still great.)

Splatter Horror

Saw

The original saw is good. Saw 2 is great; the newer ones are okay. However I am excited for the Blumhouse one. I hope it's not like the Blumhouse Halloween trilogy; Ends was so bad.
